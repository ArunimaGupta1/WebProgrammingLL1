Section1

Anew song from the web series Broken But Beautiful 3 titled Kya Kiya Hain Tune released today, May 27. The song showcases the beautiful relationship that Rumi and Agastya share. While Rumi is played by Sonia Rathee, the role of Agastya is portrayed by Sidharth Shukla. The actors began shooting for the web series in December last year. Kya Kiya Hai Tune is penned by Rashmi Virag and sung by Armaan Malik, Palak Muchhal and Amaal Mallik The music is also composed and produced by Amaal.
